## 研究背景

这个 field 从跨会议论文群里浮现，是因为传统 dense kernel 的评价方法不足以解释这些负载。Graph traversal、GNN aggregation、SpMM/SpGEMM、sparse tensor storage、point-cloud neighbor operations 和 MoE expert routing 都会把瓶颈从算术吞吐转移到索引、队列、缓存、格式和通信上。<a id="FIELD-IRREGULAR-SPARSE-001-C007"></a>[FIELD-IRREGULAR-SPARSE-001-C007](#FIELD-IRREGULAR-SPARSE-001-C007)

代表矩阵给出的早期骨架是 Gunrock、FCCM FPGA graph processing、HiCOO、RTNN 和 ZeD。它们分别指向 GPU graph runtime、FPGA graph accelerator、sparse tensor storage、neighbor search remapped to ray tracing hardware，以及 variably sparse matrix accelerator frontier。<a id="FIELD-IRREGULAR-SPARSE-001-C005"></a>[FIELD-IRREGULAR-SPARSE-001-C005](#FIELD-IRREGULAR-SPARSE-001-C005)<a id="FIELD-IRREGULAR-SPARSE-001-E001"></a>[FIELD-IRREGULAR-SPARSE-001-E001](#FIELD-IRREGULAR-SPARSE-001-E001)<a id="FIELD-IRREGULAR-SPARSE-001-E002"></a>[FIELD-IRREGULAR-SPARSE-001-E002](#FIELD-IRREGULAR-SPARSE-001-E002)<a id="FIELD-IRREGULAR-SPARSE-001-E006"></a>[FIELD-IRREGULAR-SPARSE-001-E006](#FIELD-IRREGULAR-SPARSE-001-E006)<a id="FIELD-IRREGULAR-SPARSE-001-E009"></a>[FIELD-IRREGULAR-SPARSE-001-E009](#FIELD-IRREGULAR-SPARSE-001-E009)<a id="FIELD-IRREGULAR-SPARSE-001-E020"></a>[FIELD-IRREGULAR-SPARSE-001-E020](#FIELD-IRREGULAR-SPARSE-001-E020)

cross-venue trend claim 已经给出本报告的主判断：sparse and irregular workloads 正在拆成至少三类问题，即 sparse neural/tensor modeling、graph/GNN/recommendation locality、scientific sparse/tensor-core reformulation；把它们全写成一个 irregular acceleration label 会遮蔽不同 workload 和评价方法。<a id="FIELD-IRREGULAR-SPARSE-001-C006"></a>[FIELD-IRREGULAR-SPARSE-001-C006](#FIELD-IRREGULAR-SPARSE-001-C006)

## 发展脉络

**2016-2018：图和稀疏根。** Gunrock 建立了 GPU 图runtime/库锚点，而 FCCM 图处理和 Graphicionado 显示了用于图分析的自定义硬件路线。 GraphPIM 将相同的随机访问压力转移到内存端卸载。 HiCOO 将稀疏tensor转化为存储格式问题，获得官方 SC 奖项认可。[FIELD-IRREGULAR-SPARSE-001-E001](#FIELD-IRREGULAR-SPARSE-001-E001)[FIELD-IRREGULAR-SPARSE-001-E002](#FIELD-IRREGULAR-SPARSE-001-E002)[FIELD-IRREGULAR-SPARSE-001-E004](#FIELD-IRREGULAR-SPARSE-001-E004)[FIELD-IRREGULAR-SPARSE-001-E005](#FIELD-IRREGULAR-SPARSE-001-E005)[FIELD-IRREGULAR-SPARSE-001-E006](#FIELD-IRREGULAR-SPARSE-001-E006)
